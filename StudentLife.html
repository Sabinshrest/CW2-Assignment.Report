Clubs and Societies

Nepal University boasts a wide range of clubs and societies that cater to various interests, including academic clubs, cultural societies, and hobby groups. Students are encouraged to join and participate in these groups to enhance their university experience.

Sports and Fitness

Our university offers excellent sports facilities, including a gymnasium, sports fields, and courts for various sports like football, basketball, and tennis. Regular fitness classes and sports events are organized to keep our students active and healthy.

Cultural Events

We celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Nepal through various cultural events and festivals. Students have the opportunity to experience traditional music, dance, and arts, and participate in cultural exchange programs.

Accommodation

Our on-campus accommodation provides a safe and comfortable living environment for students. With modern facilities and a supportive community, students can focus on their studies and enjoy their university life to the fullest.

Support Services

To ensure our students' well-being and success, we offer a range of support services, including academic advising, counseling, career services, and health services. Our dedicated staff is always available to assist students with any challenges they may face.
